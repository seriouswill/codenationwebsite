    About MEEEE!

    Follow @seriouswill

    Here is a little bit about me.

    My name is Will.

    I am a classically trained actor, and have worked in the industry for over 10 years. The bulk of my expereince has been in the Theatre, and as such, like many of my colleagues - am now completely without work. Parralell to my work in the theatre, I have always had a keen interest in technology, gaming, and computers at large - since tinkering around with my dad's mac at the age of 5! During the first lockdown, I began to take up an interest in coding - I began codecademy's web development course, and a plan began to formulate... I foresaw a future, a bright, shiny future, wherein I could code professionally and not have to rely on the unstable, and turbulent life of an actor!

      Other nuggets about me include:
    • I am an avid Tottenham Hotspurs fan!
    • I climbed Cotopaxi, the tallest active volcano in the world, when I was just 15.
    • I listen to a lot of heavy music, Deathcore, Hardcore, Punk. If it rips, I'm probably into it.
    • But my favourite band in the world, is Radiohead.
    I love to go on walks and play disc golf! I have come to the countryside quite late in life, but as my better half says, there is nothing better for your head than going for a good old stroll through the hills, and how lucky we are to be so close to the Peak District and the Lakes!
